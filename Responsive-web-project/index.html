History

    On 24th of April 2002, Vientiane Laos. There's where the journey towards the wonderful begins!

  • 2008-2013

    Elementary school

    I enrolled in a private school at that time called "Eastern Star Billingual School".

  • 2013-2016

    Middle school

    The same school as elementary since the school combines both elementary, middle and highschool altogether. Although same school, they did however change their name subtly to "Eastern Star Schools"

Genius Olympiad 2018 (Robotics)

I got silver medal this time!

The robot that won us the silver medal!

On June 2018, I competed in a competition "Genius Olympiad" in robotics category. The competition is located in New York, USA and it was one heck of an experience for me! Our team consisted of 6 people with 3 in each groups. I was the controller and also a partial designer of the robot.
